The road is barely lit and the light from the lone lamp post is flickering on it’s way out. A fully loaded taxi overtakes you from the left and stutters in front of you giving a heavy plume of dark smoke. And as that smoke slowly dissipates, you notice that the headlights of the taxi exist but are probably powered by a pencil cell. Light barely exists to check the box. Inside the taxi though it’s scintillating with red, green and blue lights flickering … from the radio? And then you notice there are two guys actually seated in the rear trunk, barely awake. Legs dangling out and almost touching the roads, and the trunk almost closed close up on their thighs. Everyday mundaneness. Yet another day in this little country and huge continent.
