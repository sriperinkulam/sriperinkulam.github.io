To start with I’m now married for about a year now to a then colleague of mine at TeachforIndia. Right after the wedding, Meena[Me] had plans to head to UCAL, Berkeley for her masters. Just a couple of days before she flew out, the university got back to her stating she couldn’t join that term as she didn’t have the mandatory 16 years of education.
