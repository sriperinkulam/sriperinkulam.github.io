Marrow Donor

Back in India, about eight years ago a colleague of mine sent out out an e-mail blast to the company bulletin board checking to see if anyone was a marrow donor. His son needed a transplant and the odds werent in his favor. I had no clue what that entailed and read up a bit on it.Later that evening as I tried registering on to the Be the match portal, I realized they didnt have the support framework to sign-on users outside the U.S. It didnt take a minute for me to close the tab and get back to what I was doing. A couple weeks later I learnt the kid died and somewhere deep in my heart I regretted not trying harder to sign-up or figure a way around.
