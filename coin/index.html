Over the last few years M&I have been collecting coins as we’ve moved across different posts. These MSG coins are real cool and are one of the ways the marines raise funds for the Marine ball. Not quite sure until when we’ll collect these but I did want to digitize them. Only a question of time that we start losing/gifting them.
