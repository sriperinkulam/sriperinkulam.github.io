Several years ago, maybe somewhere around 2000, I got into collecting stamps as a hobby. I digitized them in 2018, set them up in a ‘gallery’ in 2020 and eventually with all things digital, lost track of them. I’ve sort of revived that collection here, since this is one space I frequent more often. Around 2018, I also handed over my physical collection to Mummyjaan. Not quite sure if she’s actively maintaining it.
